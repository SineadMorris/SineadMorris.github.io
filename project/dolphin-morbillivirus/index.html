Disease modeling short course

 

Event: Summer bootcamp for infectious disease modeling

Location: The Institute of Statistical Mathematics, Tokyo

Event URL: https://sites.google.com/site/modelinfection/home/shortcourse4

Lecture: The purpose of my guest lecture was to introduce students to ways in which the SIR model can be applied to real disease outbreaks. I focussed on a past project that developed a statistical SIR framework to model the spatiotemporal dynamics of dolphin morbillivirus. The slides from this talk are below.
